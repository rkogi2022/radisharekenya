About Jani AI

Jani AI Kenya is an innovative program that aims to play a crucial role in addressing climate change by driving the development of technologies, strategies, solutions and integration of Public and Private partnership to reduce the impact of climate change.
Jani AI utilizes local digital solutions available to many across Kenya and Africa, including but not limited to; Use of WhatsApp, Mobile Application, & enriched Data and Reporting Systems.

Advocacy on Social Media

In July 2024, Jani AI launched an advocacy campaign on Climate Change in the Kenya via social media platforms i.e. Facebook, X (twitter), LinkedIn and Tiktok
The advocacy campaign dubbed "Hatua Yetu Future Yetu".

Campaign Communication Objectives

  • To create awareness on effects of Climate Change
  • To communication the power and impact when youth take up climate change actions
  • To increase the awareness of the utilization of Artification Inteligence & Technologies in addressing Climate Change in Kenya and Africa
  • To encourage Tree Growing at the lowest level i.e. schools, and community
  • To advocate for increased Community Based Organization Joint Network in addressing Climate Change
  • To lobby for climate change partnerships
