Madde MD LTD

Madde MD LTD (MD Limited) is a regional advisory, management & consulting, capacity building and investment firm that provides solutions in the health, research, climate change, human resource, finance, marketing, and technology/digital sectors. Madde MD boasts of working with over 200 private institutions, various county governments, and development partners across Kenya. MD Limited draws her success from a pool of experienced and talented professionals from both private and public sectors. The firm is driven by its mission to support clients grow sustainable investments and competitive businesses while at the same time responding to societal needs.
